Seasonal Guide

Seasonal Flowers You’ll Love

Discover the most beautiful seasonal blooms and how to care for them.

Seasonal flowers bring freshness, vibrant colors, and natural beauty into your home. Choosing blooms that are in season ensures better quality, longer life, and more affordable arrangements.

Spring Blooms

Spring flowers symbolize renewal and new beginnings. These blooms are soft, colorful, and full of life.

  • Tulips: Bright, elegant, and easy to maintain
  • Daffodils: Cheerful flowers that thrive in cool weather
  • Hyacinths: Known for their fragrance and rich colors

Summer Favorites

Summer flowers thrive in warm weather and bring bold, joyful colors to any space.

Seasonal flowers last longer because they grow in their natural environment.
  • Sunflowers: Bright and uplifting
  • Roses: Classic beauty available in many shades
  • Zinnias: Low-maintenance and long-lasting

Autumn Beauties

Fall flowers feature warm tones like orange, yellow, and deep red, perfectly matching the season.

  • Chrysanthemums: Hardy and long-lasting
  • Marigolds: Bright, festive, and resilient
  • Dahlias: Dramatic blooms with rich textures

Winter Elegance

Winter flowers add elegance and freshness during the colder months, often paired with greenery for a cozy feel.

  • Amaryllis: Bold and striking winter blooms
  • Poinsettias: A holiday favorite
  • Carnations: Long-lasting and widely available

How to Care for Seasonal Flowers

Proper care helps seasonal flowers stay fresh and vibrant for longer.

  • Change water every 2 days
  • Trim stems at an angle
  • Keep flowers away from direct sunlight
  • Remove wilted petals and leaves

Choosing seasonal flowers not only enhances beauty but also supports sustainability and freshness. Let nature guide your floral choices.
