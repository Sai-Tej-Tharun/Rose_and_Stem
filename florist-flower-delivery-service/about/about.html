About Stackly Florist

Flowers With Purpose & Passion

We help people celebrate life’s most meaningful moments through thoughtful floral experiences.

Our Mission

Stackly Florist was founded to simplify gifting flowers for special occasions. From anniversaries to corporate events, our platform automates reminders, subscriptions, and deliveries — so you never forget what matters most.

We believe flowers aren’t just products — they’re emotional experiences.

Our Journey

2018

Stackly Florist was founded with a simple goal: make flower gifting effortless.

2020

Launched subscription-based flower deliveries and reminder system.

2023

Expanded to 120+ partner florists with nationwide delivery coverage.

2026

Introduced customer dashboards, analytics, and smart scheduling tools.
